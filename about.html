Cardio Monitoring

This includes tools and techniques to monitor the health and activity of the heart such as heart rate, rhythm, blood pressure, oxygen saturation,etc.Cardio monitoring is often used in medical settings to help diagnose and monitor conditions such as heart disease, arrhythmias, and high blood pressure.

Medical Treatment

Medical treatment refers to various techniques, medications, and procedures to manage or cure diseases, injuries, or other medical conditions.Treatment options may include medication, surgery, physical therapy, occupational therapy, radiation therapy, chemotherapy, and other interventions.

Emergency Help

Emergency help refers to immediate assistance provided to individuals who are experiencing a sudden and unexpected medical or traumatic event that requires urgent attention.It involves providing basic life support measures such as CPR, administering oxygen, controlling bleeding,etc

First Aid

First aid refers to the initial medical assistance provided to an individual who has been injured or suddenly taken ill. The primary goal of first aid is to stabilize the person's condition and prevent the injury or illness from getting worse until they can receive further medical attention.
